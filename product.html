Face Nail Art

Fine Art Detail Brush

The backbone of face nail art — this brush allows artists to paint expressions, portraits, and character art at a miniature scale directly on the nail surface.

How It's Used

Dip the brush lightly into acrylic or gel paint, wipe excess on a palette, then use the tip to draw fine lines for eyebrows, lash lines, lip outlines, and iris detail. The long, fine bristles allow for sustained pressure control across tiny strokes. Artists commonly build faces layer by layer — skin tone, shadow, feature outlines, highlights.

Key Features
  • Bristle width: 0.5 mm – 1 mm for maximum precision
  • Long handle for steady hand control
  • Kolinsky or synthetic fibre options available
  • Works with acrylics, gel paints & nail lacquers
Precision Portraits Acrylic Paint Beginner-Friendly

Face Nail Art

Acrylic Paint Gel

A revolutionary medium that combines the pigment load of acrylic paint with the extended working time of gel, giving nail artists more time to blend and detail faces.

How It's Used

Apply a small amount to a mixing palette. Use a detailing brush to mix shades and build skin tones. Layer from light to dark — base tone first, then shadow in the hollows, highlights on raised areas like the nose bridge and brow bone. Cure under UV/LED lamp between layers for a permanent, chip-resistant finish.

Key Features
  • Extended open time — does not dry before curing
  • High pigment concentration for opacity in thin layers
  • Mixable — blend any skin tone from primary colours
  • Cures under UV or LED lamp for durability
UV/LED High Pigment Skin Tones Blendable

  • Suitable for gel, acrylic, and regular polish

Geometric Nail Art

Nail Striping Tape

Hair-thin self-adhesive metallic or matte tape used both as a masking guide and as a decorative element in geometric nail art designs.

How It's Used

For masking: apply tape to a dry base coat in the desired pattern, paint over with a second colour, wait 60 seconds, then peel the tape away before the polish fully cures to reveal clean geometric edges. For decoration: press directly onto cured gel, cut to length, and seal with a glossy top coat. The tape itself becomes a shiny line element in the final design.

Key Features
  • Width: 1 mm — creates perfectly even lines
  • Available in gold, silver, black, rose gold, holographic
  • Low-tack adhesive — peels cleanly without damage
  • Roll of 100 m — long-lasting supply
1mm Width Masking Metallic Sharp Edges

Glitter Nail Art

Fine Glitter Powder

Ultra-fine cosmetic-grade glitter particles that catch and scatter light in every direction, creating a full-sparkle nail finish unlike any polish alone.

How It's Used

While gel base is still sticky and uncured, dip a small sponge applicator or eyeshadow brush into the glitter powder and press firmly onto the nail surface. For full coverage: pack densely all over. For ombre: concentrate glitter at the tip and feather inward. Then cure under UV/LED lamp to lock in the glitter permanently. Seal with a generous layer of gel top coat, filling in all the texture.

Key Features
  • Particle size: 0.008" (ultra-fine) to 0.015" (fine)
  • Cosmetic-grade — skin safe and non-toxic
  • Available in 20+ shades including holographic and duochrome
  • Works with gel, acrylic, and regular lacquer

Abstract Nail Art

Marble Ink Drops

Water-soluble concentrated inks that spread and swirl naturally on a wet gel surface, creating organic marble-like veining patterns that are impossible to replicate by hand.

How It's Used

Apply a thin layer of uncured gel top coat (or blooming gel) to the nail. Using a dropper, release one or two drops of marble ink directly onto the wet surface. Watch as the ink naturally spreads and feathers. Use a thin brush to gently drag veins through the ink for a more controlled marble look. Once satisfied, cure the nail under UV/LED to permanently capture the pattern.

Key Features
  • 10 ink colours including classic black, grey, terracotta, and sage
  • Water-activated — spreads beautifully on wet gel
  • No two patterns ever identical
  • UV/LED stable — colour does not fade after curing
Marble Effect 10 Colours Organic Pattern UV Stable

Abstract Nail Art

Blooming Gel

A reactive clear gel medium that causes coloured polish or ink to spread, bleed, and bloom outward in flower-like bursts — the secret behind abstract watercolour nail effects.

How It's Used

Apply a thin even layer of blooming gel over a cured base and do NOT cure it — leave it sticky and wet. Immediately drop or brush small amounts of coloured polish or ink onto the blooming gel surface. The gel reacts chemically with the colour, pushing it outward in soft cloud-like bloom shapes. Add multiple colours for layered watercolour effects. When the pattern is complete, cure to freeze the design.

Key Features
  • Clear formulation — does not add opacity
  • Works with any gel polish, lacquer, or nail ink
  • Blooming speed adjustable by gel layer thickness
  • Cures under UV or LED lamp to fix the final pattern
Watercolour Effect Reactive Gel Bloom Art Unique Designs
