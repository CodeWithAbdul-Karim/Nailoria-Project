Nail Art Guide

Application, Removal & Care

  • Clean and dry the nails properly to remove oil and dust
  • Shape and file nails for a smooth base
  • Apply a thin layer of base coat to protect natural nails
  • Apply the selected nail design (floral, abstract, glitter, geometric, face art, etc.)
  • Let each layer dry properly before adding the next one
  • Seal the design with a top coat for shine and long-lasting finish
  • Allow nails to fully set before touching anything

  • Soak nails in warm water or use nail polish remover
  • Use cotton pads with acetone for strong or gel-based designs
  • Gently wipe off the design without harsh rubbing
  • Repeat if needed until all polish is removed
  • Wash hands after removal
  • Apply moisturizer or cuticle oil to restore hydration

  • Keep nails moisturized using cuticle oil or hand cream
  • Avoid excessive exposure to water and harsh chemicals
  • Wear gloves while cleaning or doing household work
  • Give nails breaks between designs to maintain strength
  • Keep nails trimmed and filed regularly
  • Maintain a healthy diet and drink enough water for strong nail growth
