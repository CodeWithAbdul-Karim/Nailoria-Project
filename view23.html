Beautiful Nails Design

Welcome to Nails Web. We provide the best nail designs with modern beauty trends. Our designs are simple, elegant and professional.


First clean your nails properly using soap and water. Dry them completely so no moisture is left. Apply a thin layer of base coat, then carefully place the nail design from the cuticle area and press it gently for 20–30 seconds. Finish with a top coat for long-lasting shine and protection. Avoid touching water for at least 1–2 hours after application for best results.

After applying nails, avoid heavy water exposure and harsh chemicals. Use cuticle oil daily to keep your nails healthy and shiny. Do not use nails as tools to open or scratch objects. Keep them clean and gently wipe with a soft cloth when needed. Proper care will make your nail design last longer and stay beautiful for days.

To remove nails safely, soak your fingers in warm water for 10–15 minutes. This will loosen the adhesive. Slowly lift the nails from the edges instead of pulling them directly. You can also use a gentle nail remover solution. After removal, wash your hands and apply moisturizer or oil to restore natural nail strength and keep them healthy.
